An Immediate Financing Arrangement is an advanced planning strategy — not a product.

Estate & Succession Planning

For many Canadian business owners and high-net-worth families, long-term wealth preservation is just as important as wealth creation. Effective estate and succession planning ensures a smooth transition of family assets, eliminates unnecessary tax exposure, and maximizes the value transferred to the next generation. Corporate-owned life insurance is one of the most powerful tools available to enhance these strategies—particularly when combined with estate freezes, family trusts, and long-term corporate planning.
